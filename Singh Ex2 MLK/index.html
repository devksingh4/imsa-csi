Fun Facts about Barack Obama

  • Obama is the 44th president of the United States, and the only African American president of the United States.
  • Obama's name means "one who is blessed" in Swahili.
  • Obama's time in office marked a "a larger percentage-point decline in the recovery, falling much faster than the overall unemployment rate over the last year [2010]".
  • Obama was named Time's Person of the Year, once in 2008 and once in 2012.
