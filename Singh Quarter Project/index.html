Inclusive and Equitable Quality Education

United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 4



What is the importance of Education?

Education is important because it allows people to be more sophisticated and make good decisions based on the ability to reason logically.
  • Ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all
    • STEM
    • Music
    • The Arts

  • Increase literacy rates, particulary for minorities.
    • While sub-Saharan Africa made the greatest progress in primary school enrolment among all developing regions – from 52 percent in 1990, up to 78 percent in 2012 – large disparities still remain.
    • It's important to ensure that everyone in the world is literate to ensure that they can thrive.
